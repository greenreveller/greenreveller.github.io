FAQs

What is careers coaching?

It’s a comprehensive 1:1 service to support and empower you to be fulfilled in your work and achieve work-life balance. It is confidential, focused time just for you. A good careers coach will listen, help you reflect, ask effective and challenging questions to help you make positive changes.

Who is careers coaching for?

Anyone! No matter what stage of your career you’re at you can benefit from coaching to help you figure out your goals. You might want to get more from your current job, explore new career possibilities, get better at networking or start your own business – my clients' needs have varied significantly.

What's your approach?

I take a proactive, person-centred approach and always work to get achievable outcomes.

Each session is focused on your needs and clearly structured so that your time is truly maximised. Clients usually book a block of 4-6 sessions to help us explore their mindset and skillset in depth and then create a detailed action plan.
